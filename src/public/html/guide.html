Evently User Guide

Master the platform with these quick-start instructions.

1
For Students

Browse the Dashboard for upcoming events. Click "Join Waitlist" if an event is full. You have 2 hours to claim a spot once promoted.

2
For EXCO Members

Use the "Create Event" button to submit proposals. Monitor your event's health in the Analytics tab and use the QR Scanner for attendance.

3
For Teachers

Access the "Approvals" dashboard to review EXCO proposals. Download the attached PDF proposal before clicking Approve or Reject.

Still need help? Ask our AI Support Expert on the dashboard!
